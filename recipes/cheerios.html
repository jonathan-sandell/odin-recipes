Cheerios With Orange Juice

A Twist On The Classic

Ever craved a bowl of cheerios, but didn't have any milk? If you have orange juice, this recipe will work in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • A bowl's worth of cheerios
  • 8 fluid ounces orange juice, preferably Florida's Natural

Steps

  1. Find a clean bowl and spoon. If none are available, just pull some dirties from the dishwasher or sink.
  2. Pour cheerios into bowl.
  3. Pour orange juice over cheerios until cheerios rise to edge of bowl.
  4. Eat with a spoon
  5. Question your life decisions
